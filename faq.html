Our Frequently Asked Questions!

Q: Where do we find our class content?

A: Each instructor writes their own unique classes each week with content that can only be found with us! This sets us apart from other studios in the area in a positive way.

Q: What should I wear to class?

A: We recommend that students wear comfortable clothes such as leggings and tank tops-- wearing sticky socks are optional per individual preference!

Q: What can I expect from taking a barre class at Barre Life?

A: We instruct a head to toe, low instensity muscle workout unlike any other. You will work areas that you didn't even know you had, and you will leave feeling strong and toned!

We would love to answer any questions that you have! Any remaining questions that have not been answered above can be answered through email or by phone-- please refer to the contact page!
